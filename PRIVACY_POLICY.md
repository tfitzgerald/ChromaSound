# Privacy Policy for ChromaSound

**Last updated:** March 29, 2026

**Developer:** Thane Fitzgerald (ThinkingThane)

## Overview

ChromaSound is a real-time audio frequency visualizer for Android. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

## What the App Does

ChromaSound captures audio from your device's microphone and processes it in real time using Fast Fourier Transform (FFT) analysis to generate a visual frequency spectrum display. All audio processing happens entirely on your device.

## Data Collection

**ChromaSound does not collect, store, record, transmit, or share any data whatsoever.** Specifically:

- **Audio data** is captured from the microphone solely for real-time visualization. Audio is processed frame-by-frame in memory and immediately discarded. No audio is ever recorded, saved, or sent anywhere.
- **Personal information** is not collected. The app does not require an account, login, or any personal details.
- **Usage analytics** are not collected. The app contains no analytics SDKs, tracking code, or telemetry of any kind.
- **Location data** is not accessed or collected.
- **Device information** is not collected or transmitted.

## Permissions

ChromaSound requests the following Android permission:

- **Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO):** Required to capture audio input for real-time frequency visualization. Without this permission, the app cannot function. Audio captured through this permission is processed on-device only and is never recorded or transmitted.

## Third-Party Services

ChromaSound does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, analytics platforms, or cloud services. The app operates entirely offline and on-device.

## Children's Privacy

ChromaSound does not collect any data from any user, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

## Data Security

Since ChromaSound does not collect, store, or transmit any data, there is no user data at risk. All audio processing occurs in volatile memory on your device and is discarded in real time.
